# Cosmic Questions
*A collection of thoughts, wonders, and existential mysteries.*

1. If Earth was our only home, why are the stars so reachable? The sheer size of the universe is not a barrier; it's a 'Welcome Mat' for the curious. Maybe we aren't just Earthlings; maybe we are Citizens of the Void.
2. Are we already living inside a Black Hole or its theoretical twin, a White Hole?
3. Did the Big Bang actually happen, or is it just a phase in an infinite cycle of the universe?
4. We have never observed a White Hole in our current universe. Is it possible that our entire Universe is the 'Output' of a massive Black Hole from a parent dimension? Could the Big Bang have been the moment a Black Hole's singularity 'rebounded' and exploded outward as a White Hole, creating our space-time?
5. We haven't discovered a single White Hole in our observable universe. Is it possible that the Universe itself originated from one final, primordial White Hole?"
